## Stratus Collection
This collection of audio visual works explores the patterns and textures found in grain clouds. Created with the aid of Touchdesigner, GLSL, and particle simulation, the works feature the real-time modular granularization of resonator models to produce a range of unique soundscapes, textures, and timbres.
Through careful programming and experimentation, GRNCH has crafted a series of audiovisual experiences that are both technically impressive and artistically engaging. The works feature dynamic particle simulations that complement the soundscape and create a mesmerizing visual experience.
Overall, this collection of audio visual works is proof of GRNCH's dedication to the craft of digital art and sound design. Its subtle and understated approach belies the technical complexity and creativity that went into its creation, resulting in a collection that is both accessible and thought-provoking.

## Etra Collection
This collection of Rutt Etra-influenced audio visual works made in Touchdesigner is a showcase of the creative potential of digital art. This series of works pays homage to the legendary Rutt Etra video synthesizer of the 1970s, which was known for its groundbreaking ability to manipulate video signals in real-time.
In this collection, GRNCH has harnessed the power of Touchdesigner to create a stunning audio-visual experience that seamlessly blends intricate geometric patterns, rich color palettes, and organic textures. The works are characterized by their hypnotic motion, as the shapes and colors constantly evolve and morph in response to the audio input.
Each piece in the collection is a unique exploration of the potential of audio-visual art, and GRNCH has carefully crafted each work to evoke a distinct emotional response. From the meditative and contemplative to the energizing and exhilarating, these works offer a range of experiences that will captivate and engage audiences of all kinds.
This collection is a testament to the power of technology to create art that transcends traditional boundaries and opens up new avenues for expression. With their sophisticated blend of audio and visual elements, these works invite us to explore the limits of our perception and to embrace the infinite possibilities of the digital realm.

## Vertex Displacement Collection
This collection of audio visual artwork showcases the captivating potential of Touchdesigner and GLSL in creating mesmerizing abstract shapes through vertex displacement. The collection is a unique exploration of form and color, with intricate details and nuanced visual effects.
Each piece in the collection features a unique arrangement of shapes, colors, and textures that dance and pulsate in response to sound and music. The audio input is processed using real-time algorithms that analyze the frequency and amplitude of the music, translating these signals into visual cues that drive the movement and shape of the abstract forms.
At the heart of each piece is the use of vertex displacement, which creates a sense of depth and movement within each shape. This technique allows for a level of visual complexity and detail that would be impossible to achieve through traditional 2D rendering techniques.
The shapes themselves are beautifully abstract, with flowing lines, sharp angles, and geometric patterns. Each piece is a unique exploration of form and color, with the vertex displacement bringing an added dimensionality and richness to the visuals.
The use of GLSL adds an additional layer of complexity to the artwork, allowing for the creation of intricate and nuanced visual effects. The interplay of light and shadow, the subtle shifting of colors, and the sense of depth and movement are all made possible through the use of this powerful programming language.
The resulting artworks represent evidence of the creative possibilities of Touchdesigner and GLSL, showcasing a harmonious marriage between art and technology. Viewers are transported into a world of vibrant colors, swirling patterns, and hypnotic motion, immersing themselves in a sensory experience that is both captivating and otherworldly.

## Julia Fractal Collection
The collection of audio visual artworks made in Touchdesigner using RayTK and Julia Fractals is a stunning display of computational art that explores the beauty and complexity of fractal geometry. Each piece in the collection features a mesmerizing arrangement of intricate patterns and shapes that are generated using the Julia set fractal algorithm.
RayTK is a raymarching shader toolkit for TouchDesigner that allows artists to create complex 3D shapes and textures in real-time. Using this powerful tool, the fractal patterns are rendered and manipulated in a dynamic and interactive way, responding to sound and music in real-time. The audio input is analyzed and translated into visual cues that drive the movement and shape of the fractal patterns, creating a mesmerizing audio-visual experience.
The resulting artworks are a breathtaking example of the beauty and complexity of fractal geometry, demonstrating the incredible possibilities of computational art and real-time visual processing. Viewers are transported into a world of vibrant colors, swirling patterns, and intricate shapes, immersing themselves in a sensory experience that is both captivating and awe-inspiring.

## Strange Attraktor Collection
The collection of audio visual artworks made in Touchdesigner based on particle displacement using Attraktors Designer is a stunning and mesmerizing display of generative art. Each piece is a unique and ever-evolving composition of swirling particles, moving in patterns that are both intricate and unpredictable.
The use of Attraktors Designer allows for the manipulation of these particles with different equations of strange attractors, which can be mixed and designed in various ways to create aesthetic and coherent shapes. The resulting movements of the particles are controlled by audio or external controllers, resulting in original and constantly evolving compositions.
The beauty of these artworks lies in the harmony between the visual and audio elements, with the movements of the particles perfectly synced to the accompanying soundscapes. The particles seem to dance and flow in response to the music, creating a captivating and immersive experience for the viewer.
Overall, the collection of audio visual artworks made in Touchdesigner using Attraktors Designer is a demonstration of the power of generative art, showcasing the beauty and complexity that can arise from the interplay of mathematics, technology, and creativity.
